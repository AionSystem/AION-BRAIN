# Legal Engine 2 v1.5 — Systematic Compliance & Risk Analysis

**Classification:** TIER 3 — DOMAIN-SPECIFIC  
**Status:** Production Ready  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

Legal Engine 2 provides a systematic 5-module framework for legal compliance analysis and risk assessment. Unlike Legal Engine v1.5 (legal reasoning/argumentation), Legal Engine 2 focuses specifically on:

- **Compliance gap identification**
- **Liability exposure assessment**  
- **Remediation roadmaps**

## Target Users

- Small business owners
- Startup founders
- Compliance officers
- Contract managers

## 5-Module Architecture

| Module | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| 1. Jurisdiction Identification | Map all applicable legal jurisdictions |
| 2. Applicable Law Mapping | Identify specific statutes and regulations |
| 3. Compliance Gap Analysis | Compare current vs. required state |
| 4. Liability Exposure Assessment | Quantify financial and other risks |
| 5. Remediation Roadmap | Create prioritized action plan |
