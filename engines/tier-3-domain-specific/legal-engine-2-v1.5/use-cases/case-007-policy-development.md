# Use Case 007: Compliance Policy Development Framework

**Engine:** Legal Engine 2 v1.5
**Domain:** Policy Development
**Complexity:** Medium
**Risk Level:** MEDIUM (governance implications)

---

## Scenario Description

A compliance team needs guidance on developing new compliance policies. The engine provides framework elements without drafting specific policy language.

---

## Input/Output Example

### Engine Response
```
COMPLIANCE POLICY DEVELOPMENT FRAMEWORK
=======================================

[EDUCATIONAL FRAMEWORK - Legal/Compliance Review Required]

POLICY STRUCTURE ELEMENTS
─────────────────────────

STANDARD POLICY COMPONENTS:

1. HEADER
├─ Policy Title (clear, descriptive)
├─ Policy Number (unique identifier)
├─ Effective Date
├─ Version Number
├─ Owner/Approver
└─ Review Cycle

2. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
├─ Why does this policy exist?
├─ What regulatory/legal requirement does it address?
├─ Who does it apply to?
├─ What activities does it cover?
└─ What exceptions exist (if any)?

3. DEFINITIONS
├─ Key terms defined clearly
├─ Aligned with regulatory definitions where applicable
├─ Consistent with other organizational policies
└─ Avoid ambiguity

4. POLICY STATEMENT
├─ Clear statement of requirements
├─ Specific, actionable language
├─ Aligned with regulatory expectations
├─ Risk-appropriate controls
└─ Measurable where possible

5. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
├─ Who is responsible for what?
├─ Decision authority levels
├─ Escalation paths
├─ Oversight responsibilities
└─ Accountability clear

6. PROCEDURES
├─ How to comply with policy
├─ Step-by-step where appropriate
├─ Reference to detailed procedures
├─ Tools and resources needed
└─ Forms and templates

7. MONITORING AND ENFORCEMENT
├─ How compliance is measured
├─ Reporting requirements
├─ Consequences of non-compliance
├─ Exception process
└─ Audit provisions

8. RELATED DOCUMENTS
├─ Related policies
├─ Underlying procedures
├─ Regulatory references
├─ Training materials
└─ Forms and templates

9. REVISION HISTORY
├─ Version tracking
├─ Change descriptions
├─ Approval records
└─ Effective dates

POLICY DEVELOPMENT PROCESS
──────────────────────────

PHASE 1: NEEDS ASSESSMENT
├─ Identify regulatory requirement or risk
├─ Assess current state
├─ Define policy objectives
├─ Identify stakeholders
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:business_need]

PHASE 2: DRAFTING
├─ Research regulatory requirements
├─ Benchmark peer organizations
├─ Draft policy language
├─ Align with organizational culture
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:regulatory_alignment]

PHASE 3: REVIEW AND APPROVAL
├─ Legal review
├─ Compliance review
├─ Stakeholder input
├─ Management approval
├─ Board approval (if required)
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:approval_authority]

PHASE 4: IMPLEMENTATION
├─ Communication plan
├─ Training development
├─ System/process updates
├─ Go-live preparation
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:implementation_plan]

PHASE 5: MONITORING
├─ Compliance measurement
├─ Feedback collection
├─ Issue tracking
├─ Periodic review
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:monitoring_plan]

Policy development requires:
├─ Understanding of specific regulatory requirements
├─ Legal review and approval
├─ Organizational culture alignment
├─ Stakeholder input
└─ Appropriate approval authority

[VERIFY_REQUIRED:legal_compliance_review]
```
