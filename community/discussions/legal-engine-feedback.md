## Known Limitations

The Legal Engine v2.1 has known limitations:

| Limitation | Description | Mitigation |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Not legal advice** | Cannot provide legal advice | Always emphasizes attorney consultation |
| **US focus** | Primarily US law | Working on international expansion |
| **Currency** | Law changes rapidly | Recommends verification |
| **Specialty areas** | Limited specialty depth | Prioritizing common areas |
| **Local rules** | May not cover local court rules | Recommends local counsel |

## Ethics Considerations

### UPL Safeguards

The Legal Engine includes safeguards against unauthorized practice:

| Safeguard | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Disclaimers** | Clear statements that output is not legal advice |
| **Attorney referral** | Recommends consulting licensed attorneys |
| **Jurisdiction awareness** | Notes jurisdictional variations |
| **Privilege warnings** | Notes confidentiality considerations |

### Professional Responsibility

The engine is designed with ABA Model Rules awareness:

| Rule | Consideration |
|------|---------------|
| 1.1 Competence | Emphasizes professional judgment |
| 1.4 Communication | Supports client communication |
| 1.6 Confidentiality | Warns about data handling |
| 5.5 UPL | Prevents unauthorized practice |
