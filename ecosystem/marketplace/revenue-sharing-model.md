# Revenue Sharing Model

Framework for monetizing premium AION-BRAIN marketplace offerings.

---

## Important Notice

**The core AION-BRAIN engines are and will remain free and open source.**

This revenue model applies only to:
- Premium extensions and integrations
- Commercial services
- Enterprise-grade offerings

---

## Current Status

**Status:** Planning Phase

Revenue sharing is not yet active. This document outlines the planned model for when premium offerings are introduced.

## Revenue Share Model (Planned)

### Standard Split

| Recipient | Share | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| **Creator** | 70% | Primary value creator |
| **AION-BRAIN** | 20% | Ecosystem maintenance |
| **Community Fund** | 10% | Community development |

### Example Calculation

| Scenario | Price | Creator | AION-BRAIN | Community |
|----------|-------|---------|------------|-----------|
| $10 extension | $10 | $7.00 | $2.00 | $1.00 |
| $100/mo service | $100 | $70.00 | $20.00 | $10.00 |
| $1,000 enterprise | $1,000 | $700 | $200 | $100 |

## Getting Started as Creator

### Step 1: Create Value

- Build an extension, integration, or service
- Ensure quality standards are met
- Document thoroughly

### Step 2: Choose Model

- Decide on pricing model
- Set appropriate pricing
- Define what's included

### Step 3: Submit

- Follow submission guidelines
- Indicate pricing preference
- Provide payment information

### Step 4: Launch

- Submission approved and listed
- Promotion to community
- Sales begin

### Step 5: Earn

- Receive monthly payouts
- Track sales in dashboard
- Reinvest in improvements
