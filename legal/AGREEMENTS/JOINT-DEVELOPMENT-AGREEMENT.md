### ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Background IP"** means any intellectual property owned or controlled by a party prior to the Effective Date or developed independently outside the scope of this Agreement.

**1.2 "Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed by one party to another in connection with this Agreement.

**1.3 "Development Plan"** means the plan for development of the Joint Product as set forth in Exhibit A.

**1.4 "Foreground IP"** means any intellectual property conceived, created, or reduced to practice in the performance of the Development Plan.

**1.5 "Joint IP"** means Foreground IP created jointly by personnel of both parties where the contribution of each party is not separately identifiable.

**1.6 "Joint Product"** means the product, technology, or solution to be developed pursuant to this Agreement as described in the Development Plan.

**1.7 "Project Manager"** means the individual designated by each party to manage its participation in the development effort.

**1.8 "Sole IP"** means Foreground IP created solely by personnel of one party without contribution from the other party.

**1.9 "Steering Committee"** means the governance body established to oversee the development effort.

### ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**6.1 Background IP.**

(a) Each party retains all right, title, and interest in its Background IP

(b) No license to Background IP is granted except as expressly set forth herein

(c) Each party grants the other a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use its Background IP solely as necessary for the development effort

**6.2 Sole IP.**

(a) Sole IP shall be owned by the party whose personnel created it

(b) The creating party grants the other party a `[non-exclusive / exclusive]` license to use Sole IP for `[PURPOSE]`

**6.3 Joint IP Ownership.**

`[INSTRUCTION: Select appropriate option]`

**Option A - Equal Joint Ownership:**
Joint IP shall be jointly owned by the parties in equal, undivided shares. Each party may use and exploit Joint IP independently without accounting to the other, except that neither party may grant exclusive licenses or assign its interest without the other's consent.

**Option B - Proportional Ownership:**
Joint IP shall be owned proportionally based on contribution, as determined by the Steering Committee. Commercialization requires agreement of parties holding at least `[PERCENTAGE]`% ownership.

**Option C - Field-of-Use Allocation:**
Joint IP shall be jointly owned, but exclusive field-of-use rights are allocated as follows:

| Field | Exclusive Rights |
|-------|-----------------|
| `[FIELD 1]` | Party A |
| `[FIELD 2]` | Party B |
| All other fields | Joint (non-exclusive) |

**6.4 IP Disclosure.** Each party shall promptly disclose to the other any potentially patentable invention arising from the development effort.

**6.5 Patent Prosecution.**

(a) For Sole IP, the owning party has sole discretion over patent prosecution

(b) For Joint IP, patent prosecution decisions require mutual agreement

(c) Costs of Joint IP patent prosecution shall be shared per the cost-sharing ratio

(d) If one party declines to participate in prosecution, the other may proceed at its own cost and acquire sole ownership

**6.6 Open-Source Considerations.**

If parties agree to release Joint Product or components as open-source:

(a) Apache License 2.0 is preferred for AION-BRAIN compatibility

(b) Open-source release requires Steering Committee approval

(c) Each party must have authority for such release
