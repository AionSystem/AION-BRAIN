# Scientific Engine Feedback

Dedicated space for feedback on AION-BRAIN's Scientific Research Engine and research methodology frameworks.

---

## About the Scientific Engine

The **Scientific Research Engine** provides research methodology frameworks with:
- Peer review simulation capabilities
- Statistical analysis validation
- Reproducibility frameworks
- Methodology assessment tools
- Evidence quality evaluation

## Known Limitations

The Scientific Engine has known limitations:

| Limitation | Description | Mitigation |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Not a statistician** | Cannot replace statistical expertise | Recommends statistical consultation |
| **Field-specific** | May miss domain-specific considerations | Notes field limitations |
| **Method currency** | Methods evolve rapidly | Recommends literature review |
| **Complexity** | Cannot handle all research complexities | Recommends expert consultation |
