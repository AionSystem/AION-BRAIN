## Known Limitations

The Cinematic Engine has known limitations:

| Limitation | Description | Mitigation |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Not creative replacement** | Cannot replace human creativity | Enhances, not replaces |
| **Brand-specific** | Requires brand adaptation | Notes customization needs |
| **Platform-specific** | Platforms evolve rapidly | Recommends platform research |
| **Cultural context** | May miss cultural nuances | Recommends cultural review |
