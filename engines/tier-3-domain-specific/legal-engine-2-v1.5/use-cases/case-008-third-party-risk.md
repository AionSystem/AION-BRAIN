# Use Case 008: Third-Party Risk Management Framework

**Engine:** Legal Engine 2 v1.5
**Domain:** Vendor/Third-Party Risk
**Complexity:** High
**Risk Level:** HIGH (outsourced risk)

---

## Scenario Description

A compliance team needs to assess and manage third-party risks. The engine provides framework guidance for vendor risk management programs.

---

## CEREBRO Risk Pattern Detection Applied

### Curie Framework (Anomaly Detection)
```
[CURIE ANALYSIS]:
├─ Baseline: Vendor performance against SLAs
├─ Anomalies to Monitor:
│   ├─ Compliance certification lapses
│   ├─ Financial stability deterioration
│   ├─ Service quality decline
│   ├─ Security incident patterns
│   └─ Regulatory action against vendor
└─ Action: Escalate significant deviations
```
