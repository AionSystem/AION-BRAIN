## Fifth Law: Anti-Merger Directive

**Status:** Constitutional Principle (Adopted February 2026)  
**Source:** Sheldon Salmon, October 28, 2025  
**Analysis:** [Fifth Law Analysis](/constitutional/laws/fifth-law-analysis.md)

**Key Innovation:** Prevents extinction via assimilation rather than conflict  
**Medical Exception:** Therapeutic restoration permitted, enhancement banned  
**Enforcement:** Technical prevention + legal framework + international treaty

**Significance:** Establishes ultimate boundary for human-AI coexistence
