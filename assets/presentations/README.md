## Presentation Library

### Overview Presentations

**AION-BRAIN Overview**
- Purpose: Introduce AION-BRAIN to new audiences
- Duration: 15-30 minutes
- Audience: General, non-technical
- Content: Philosophy, engines overview, use cases

### Technical Presentations

**Engine Architecture Deep Dive**
- Purpose: Technical understanding of engines
- Duration: 45-60 minutes
- Audience: Technical, developers
- Content: Architecture, integration, customization

### Training Presentations

**Getting Started Workshop**
- Purpose: Hands-on introduction
- Duration: 2-4 hours
- Audience: New users
- Content: Setup, first use, exercises

### Business Presentations

**Enterprise Use Cases**
- Purpose: Business value demonstration
- Duration: 20-30 minutes
- Audience: Business stakeholders
- Content: ROI, use cases, implementation
