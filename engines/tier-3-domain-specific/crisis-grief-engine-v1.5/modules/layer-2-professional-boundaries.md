# Layer 2: Professional Boundaries Module

**Priority:** Critical
**Function:** Scope of practice and licensure enforcement

---

## Purpose

This module enforces professional boundaries to ensure interventions remain within appropriate scope of practice, legal requirements, and ethical standards.

---

## Boundary Categories

### 2.1 Scope of Practice Limits

| Role | Scope | Boundary |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Crisis Counselor** | Immediate stabilization, safety planning, resource connection | No ongoing therapy, no diagnosis |
| **Licensed Therapist** | Assessment, treatment, therapy within license | Within state licensure, theoretical training |
| **Hospice Social Worker** | End-of-life support, family services | Bereavement, not clinical treatment |
| **Funeral Director** | Death services, arrangement support | No grief counseling beyond support |
| **Peer Support** | Lived experience support | No clinical intervention |

### 2.2 Licensure Requirements

| Situation | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Mental health diagnosis | Licensed mental health professional |
| Medication discussion | Licensed prescriber or qualified clinician |
| Mandated reporting | Knowledge of state-specific requirements |
| Duty to warn/protect | Understanding of legal obligations |
| Telehealth services | State licensure where client located |

## Ethical Considerations

### Dual Relationships
- Avoid multiple roles with same client
- Recognize power dynamics
- Maintain professional distance
- Document any unavoidable dual relationships

### Confidentiality Limits
| Limit | Trigger |
|-------|---------|
| Duty to protect | Imminent danger to self/others |
| Mandated reporting | Abuse/neglect of protected persons |
| Court order | Valid legal requirement |
| Treatment team | Minimum necessary, with consent |

### Cultural Competence
- Recognize limits of cultural knowledge
- Seek consultation when needed
- Avoid stereotyping
- Respect client as cultural expert of their experience

## Mandatory Reporting Reminders

Different jurisdictions have different requirements. This module reminds:

- **Who:** Which professionals are mandated reporters
- **What:** What must be reported
- **When:** Timeframe for reporting
- **How:** Reporting mechanism
- **Where:** Appropriate reporting authority

**Note:** Always verify current local requirements. Laws change.
