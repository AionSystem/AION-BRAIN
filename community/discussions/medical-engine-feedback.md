# Medical Engine Feedback

Dedicated space for feedback on AION-BRAIN's Medical Engine v2.6 and healthcare-related engines.

## About the Medical Engine

**Medical Engine v2.6** provides clinical decision support frameworks with:
- Integration with UpToDate, FDA, CMS resources
- PHI redaction protocols
- Patient safety guardrails
- Clinical workflow integration
- Evidence-based medicine principles

## Known Limitations

The Medical Engine v2.6 has known limitations:

| Limitation | Description | Mitigation |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Not diagnostic** | Cannot diagnose conditions | Always emphasizes professional consultation |
| **US-centric** | Primarily US guidelines | Working on international expansion |
| **Currency** | Information may not be current | Recommends verification |
| **Specialties** | Limited specialty depth | Prioritizing common conditions |
