## Becoming a Partner

### Eligibility

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Relevant expertise** | AI, consulting, or domain expertise |
| **Technical capability** | Ability to implement solutions |
| **Alignment** | Commitment to AION principles |
| **References** | Professional references |

### Process

1. **Apply** — Submit partner application
2. **Review** — We assess fit and capability
3. **Training** — Complete partner training
4. **Certification** — Pass partner certification
5. **Launch** — Listed in partner directory

### Application

**Email:** AIONSYSTEM@outlook.com
**Subject:** "Implementation Partner Application"

Include:
- Organization overview
- Relevant experience
- Target market/domain
- Why you want to partner
