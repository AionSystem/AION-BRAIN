## Use Case Summaries

### Case 001: Fintech Product Launch Compliance
**Scenario:** Digital lending startup launching consumer loan product in 15 states
**Regulations:** State lending laws, TILA/Reg Z, ECOA/Reg B, FCRA/Reg V, state licensing
**Engine Output:** Multi-jurisdictional licensing roadmap, disclosure requirements matrix, fair lending compliance program outline

### Case 002: Multi-State Employment Expansion
**Scenario:** Tech company expanding from 1 to 12 states, growing from 25 to 150 employees
**Regulations:** Federal employment law thresholds, state-specific requirements, payroll tax, workers' comp
**Engine Output:** State-by-state compliance checklist, threshold trigger analysis, HR policy update requirements

### Case 003: Healthcare Data Platform
**Scenario:** SaaS platform processing healthcare data for multiple covered entities
**Regulations:** HIPAA, state health privacy, FDA (if clinical), FTC, state breach notification
**Engine Output:** BAA requirements, security rule compliance framework, breach response protocol

### Case 004: Environmental Permit Application
**Scenario:** Manufacturing facility seeking air quality permit in regulated state
**Regulations:** Clean Air Act, state implementation plan, local requirements, EPA guidance
**Engine Output:** Permit application roadmap, emission limits analysis, compliance monitoring plan

### Case 005: Cross-Border E-Commerce
**Scenario:** US retailer selling to EU consumers
**Regulations:** GDPR, EU consumer protection, customs/tariffs, VAT, product safety
**Engine Output:** International compliance matrix, data transfer mechanisms, consumer rights obligations

### Case 006: Government Contract Compliance
**Scenario:** Small business pursuing first federal contract
**Regulations:** FAR, agency-specific supplements, small business requirements, ethics rules
**Engine Output:** Contractor responsibility requirements, compliance program elements, certification analysis

### Case 007: Startup Regulatory Assessment
**Scenario:** Pre-launch startup assessing regulatory landscape
**Regulations:** Industry-dependent comprehensive scan
**Engine Output:** Regulatory risk assessment, licensing requirements, compliance roadmap

### Case 008: M&A Regulatory Due Diligence
**Scenario:** Acquiring company assessing target's regulatory compliance status
**Regulations:** Target's full regulatory inventory
**Engine Output:** Compliance gap analysis, liability assessment, remediation requirements

### Case 009: Data Privacy Compliance Program
**Scenario:** Mid-sized company building comprehensive privacy program
**Regulations:** CCPA/CPRA, state privacy laws, FTC, industry-specific
**Engine Output:** Privacy program framework, policy templates, vendor management requirements

### Case 010: AI/ML System Regulatory Analysis
**Scenario:** Company deploying AI system in regulated industry
**Regulations:** FTC AI guidance, EEOC AI hiring rules, state AI laws, industry-specific
**Engine Output:** AI governance framework, bias testing requirements, disclosure obligations

## Use Case Format

Each detailed use case follows this structure:

1. **Scenario Description** — Business context and regulatory question
2. **Initial Request (Problematic)** — How user might naively ask
3. **Risk Assessment** — Why naive request is risky
4. **Engine-Processed Request** — Properly structured query
5. **Engine Output** — Comprehensive regulatory analysis
6. **Verification Checklist** — Items requiring human review
7. **Key Lessons** — Takeaways from the scenario
