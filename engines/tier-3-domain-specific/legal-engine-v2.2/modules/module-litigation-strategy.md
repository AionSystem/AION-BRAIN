# Litigation Strategy Module (LSM-v1.0)

**Classification:** Legal Engine v2.2 Enhancement Module
**Purpose:** Strategic litigation analysis and case development support

---

## 1. Module Overview

The Litigation Strategy Module provides frameworks for case assessment, discovery planning, motion strategy, settlement evaluation, and trial preparation support.

### Core Functions

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Case Assessment | Evaluate strengths, weaknesses, risks |
| Discovery Planning | Strategic discovery roadmap |
| Motion Strategy | Identify dispositive opportunities |
| Settlement Analysis | BATNA and settlement range |
| Trial Preparation | Theme development and witness preparation |

## 3. Discovery Strategy

### 3.1 Discovery Planning Matrix

| Phase | Objectives | Tools | Timeline |
|-------|------------|-------|----------|
| Initial | Develop theory, identify witnesses | Initial disclosures, document requests | Early |
| Core | Build evidence record | Depositions, interrogatories | Middle |
| Expert | Technical support | Expert disclosures, reports | Per schedule |
| Rebuttal | Counter opponent's case | Rebuttal experts, targeted discovery | Late |

### 5.2 Settlement Timing Analysis

| Phase | Settlement Probability | Typical Range | Considerations |
|-------|----------------------|---------------|----------------|
| Pre-suit | Lower | Lower | Uncertainty, no legal fees |
| Post-complaint | Medium | Medium | Stakes clearer |
| Post-discovery | Higher | Higher | Evidence known |
| Pre-trial | Highest | Varies | Certainty vs. risk |
| During trial | Variable | Variable | Verdict risk |
