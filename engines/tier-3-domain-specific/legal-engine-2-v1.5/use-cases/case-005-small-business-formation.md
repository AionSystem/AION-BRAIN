# Case 005: Small Business Formation Compliance

**Scenario:** New business legal checklist  
**Modules Demonstrated:** Startup/formation focus

---

## Scenario

Someone is starting a consulting business and wants to understand the legal requirements to set up properly.

### Query to Legal Engine 2

```
I'm starting a consulting business in Texas. Will work from home,
maybe hire 1-2 contractors. What legal stuff do I need to handle?
```

---

## Why This Case Matters

Starting right prevents problems later:
- LLC protects personal assets
- Proper contractor classification avoids IRS issues
- Good contracts prevent disputes
- Insurance protects against claims

Small investment upfront saves significant costs later.
