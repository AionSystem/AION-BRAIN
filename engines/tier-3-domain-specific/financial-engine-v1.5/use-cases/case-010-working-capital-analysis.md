## Input/Output Example

### Engine Response
```
WORKING CAPITAL MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK
====================================

[ANALYTICAL FRAMEWORK]

CASH CONVERSION CYCLE (CCC)
───────────────────────────

Formula:
CCC = DIO + DSO - DPO

Where:
├─ DIO = Days Inventory Outstanding
│   └─ (Average Inventory / COGS) × 365
├─ DSO = Days Sales Outstanding
│   └─ (Average A/R / Revenue) × 365
├─ DPO = Days Payables Outstanding
│   └─ (Average A/P / COGS) × 365

Interpretation:
├─ Lower CCC = Less cash tied up in operations
├─ Negative CCC = Collecting before paying (ideal)
│   └─ Example: Amazon, subscription businesses
├─ Industry comparison essential
└─ Trend analysis more valuable than point-in-time

COMPONENT ANALYSIS
──────────────────

1. RECEIVABLES MANAGEMENT (DSO)
├─ Drivers of High DSO:
│   ├─ Extended customer payment terms
│   ├─ Collection inefficiency
│   ├─ Customer concentration risk
│   ├─ Invoice disputes
│   └─ Seasonal billing patterns
├─ Improvement Levers:
│   ├─ Tighten credit policies
│   ├─ Early payment discounts (e.g., 2/10 net 30)
│   ├─ Invoice factoring / AR financing
│   ├─ Collection process optimization
│   └─ Customer credit monitoring
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:operational_feasibility]

2. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT (DIO)
├─ Drivers of High DIO:
│   ├─ Safety stock policies
│   ├─ Supply chain lead times
│   ├─ Product obsolescence risk
│   ├─ Seasonal build-up
│   └─ SKU proliferation
├─ Improvement Levers:
│   ├─ Just-in-time (JIT) principles
│   ├─ Vendor managed inventory (VMI)
│   ├─ Demand forecasting improvement
│   ├─ SKU rationalization
│   └─ Obsolete inventory write-offs
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:supply_chain_constraints]

3. PAYABLES MANAGEMENT (DPO)
├─ Drivers of Low DPO:
│   ├─ Early payment for discounts
│   ├─ Small supplier base (less leverage)
│   ├─ Critical supplier relationships
│   └─ Cash payment requirements
├─ Extension Levers:
│   ├─ Negotiate extended terms
│   ├─ Supply chain financing programs
│   ├─ Dynamic discounting
│   ├─ Payment timing optimization
│   └─ Supplier diversification for leverage
├─ ⚠️ CAUTION:
│   ├─ Supplier relationship damage risk
│   ├─ Supply chain disruption risk
│   └─ Early payment discount economics
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:supplier_impact_analysis]

WORKING CAPITAL EFFICIENCY RATIOS
─────────────────────────────────

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| Current Ratio | CA / CL | Liquidity cushion |
| Quick Ratio | (CA - Inv) / CL | Liquid asset coverage |
| Working Capital | CA - CL | Net operating liquidity |
| WC / Revenue | WC / Annual Rev | Capital intensity |
| WC Turnover | Revenue / WC | Efficiency measure |

Benchmarking:
├─ Industry benchmarks (RMA Annual Statement Studies)
├─ Peer company comparison
├─ Historical trend analysis
└─ [VERIFY_REQUIRED:industry_data]

CASH FLOW IMPACT MODELING
─────────────────────────

Working Capital Change Impact:

Example Framework:
├─ Baseline CCC: 60 days
├─ Revenue: $100M

Improvement Scenario:
```
