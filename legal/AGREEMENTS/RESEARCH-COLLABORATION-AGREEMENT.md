**1.3 "Foreground IP"** means any intellectual property conceived, created, or reduced to practice in the performance of the Project.

**1.4 "Joint IP"** means Foreground IP created jointly by personnel of more than one party where the contribution of each party is not separately identifiable.

**1.5 "Principal Investigator" or "PI"** means the lead researcher designated by each party to direct and manage that party's participation in the Project.

### ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**6.1 Background IP.** Each party retains all right, title, and interest in its Background IP. No license to Background IP is granted except as expressly set forth herein.

**6.2 Background IP License.** Each party grants the other party a non-exclusive, royalty-free, non-transferable license to use its Background IP solely to the extent necessary to perform the Project.

**6.3 Foreground IP Ownership.**

`[INSTRUCTION: Select appropriate option]`

**Option A - Inventor Ownership:**
Foreground IP shall be owned by the party whose personnel conceived or created it. Joint IP shall be jointly owned by the parties.

**Option B - Institutional Ownership:**
Foreground IP shall be owned by the party employing the inventor(s). Joint IP shall be jointly owned by the parties.

**Option C - Project-Specific Allocation:**
Foreground IP shall be allocated as set forth in Exhibit B (IP Allocation).

**6.4 Joint IP.** Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

(a) Joint IP shall be jointly owned by the contributing parties

(b) Each joint owner may use and license Joint IP without consent of the other owner

(c) Revenues from Joint IP licensing shall be shared `[PERCENTAGE SPLIT - e.g., equally, proportionally]`

(d) No joint owner may assign its interest without consent of the other owner

**6.5 IP Disclosure.** Each party shall promptly disclose to the other party any potentially patentable invention arising from the Project.

**6.6 Patent Prosecution.**

(a) The party owning an invention shall have the first right to file patent applications

(b) If the owning party declines to file, the other party may request the right to file

(c) Costs of patent prosecution shall be borne by the filing party unless otherwise agreed

**6.7 Open-Source Considerations.**

If Project outputs will be released as open-source software:

(a) The parties shall agree on an appropriate open-source license

(b) Apache License 2.0 is preferred for compatibility with AION-BRAIN

(c) Each party represents it has authority to contribute to open-source releases

### ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY

**7.1 Confidential Treatment.** Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but not less than reasonable care.

**7.2 Permitted Disclosures.** Confidential Information may be disclosed to:

(a) Research Personnel with a need to know

(b) Institutional officials as required for oversight

(c) Funding agencies as required by grant terms

(d) As required by law or regulation

**7.3 Exclusions.** Information is not Confidential Information if it:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available without breach

(b) Was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure

(c) Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information

(d) Is received from a third party without restriction

**7.4 Duration.** Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for `[NUMBER - typically 3-5]` years.
