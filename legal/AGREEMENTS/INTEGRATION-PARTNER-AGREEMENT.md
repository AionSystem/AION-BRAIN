### ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Authorized Products"** means the Company Products that Partner is authorized to `[resell / integrate / refer]` as specified in Exhibit A.

**1.2 "Commission"** means fees payable to Partner as specified in the compensation section.

**1.3 "Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed by either party.

**1.4 "Customer"** means an end user who acquires or uses Authorized Products through Partner.

**1.5 "Integrated Solution"** means a product or service that incorporates or interfaces with Authorized Products.

**1.6 "Marks"** means trademarks, service marks, logos, and brand elements of each party.

**1.7 "Partner Program"** means Company's partner program as described in Exhibit B.

**1.8 "Referral"** means a qualified lead provided by Partner to Company.

**1.9 "Territory"** means the geographic area in which Partner may operate as specified above.

### ARTICLE 2: APPOINTMENT AND SCOPE

**2.1 Appointment.** Company hereby appoints Partner as a `[non-exclusive / exclusive]` `[reseller / integrator / referral partner]` for the Authorized Products in the Territory.

**2.2 Partner Type.**

`[INSTRUCTION: Select applicable partner type]`

**Option A - Reseller Partner:**
Partner is authorized to resell Authorized Products directly to Customers under Company's license terms.

**Option B - Integration Partner:**
Partner is authorized to integrate Authorized Products into Partner's Integrated Solutions.

**Option C - Referral Partner:**
Partner is authorized to refer potential Customers to Company for direct engagement.

**Option D - OEM Partner:**
Partner is authorized to embed Authorized Products into Partner's products under private label.

### ARTICLE 3: PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 General Obligations.** Partner shall:

(a) Actively promote and market Authorized Products in the Territory

(b) Maintain qualified sales and technical personnel

(c) Provide first-level customer support as specified

(d) Comply with Partner Program requirements

(e) Achieve minimum performance requirements

(f) Protect Company's intellectual property and Marks

(g) Operate in compliance with applicable laws

**3.2 Sales Obligations.** Partner shall:

(a) Present Authorized Products professionally and accurately

(b) Not make unauthorized representations about products

(c) Refer complex technical inquiries to Company

(d) Maintain accurate customer records

**3.3 Technical Obligations.** Partner shall:

(a) Complete required training and certifications

(b) Maintain `[NUMBER]` certified personnel

(c) Follow integration guidelines and best practices

(d) Report bugs and issues to Company

**3.4 Minimum Requirements.** Partner commits to:

| Metric | Minimum Requirement | Measurement Period |
|--------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Revenue | `$[AMOUNT]` | `[ANNUAL / QUARTERLY]` |
| Certifications | `[NUMBER]` certified staff | Ongoing |
| Customer Satisfaction | `[PERCENTAGE]`% satisfaction | `[PERIOD]` |

**3.5 Prohibited Activities.** Partner shall not:

(a) Make unauthorized modifications to Authorized Products

(b) Engage in deceptive marketing practices

(c) Sell outside the Territory without authorization

(d) Assign rights to third parties without consent

(e) Compete directly with Company in the Territory

(f) Disparage Company or Authorized Products

(c) Technical documentation and resources

(e) Second-level technical support

**4.2 Product Updates.** Company shall:

(a) Provide reasonable notice of product updates

(c) Maintain backward compatibility where feasible

**4.3 Lead Referrals.** Company may refer leads to Partner in the Territory at Company's discretion.

### ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND MARKS

**6.1 License to Marks.** Company grants Partner a non-exclusive license to use Company's Marks solely for:

(a) Marketing Authorized Products

(b) Identifying Partner's relationship with Company

(c) Purposes authorized under the Partner Program

**6.2 Mark Usage Requirements.** Partner shall:

(a) Follow Company's brand guidelines (Exhibit C)

(b) Obtain approval for marketing materials using Marks

(c) Not modify or alter Marks

(d) Include required trademark notices

(e) Not register or claim rights to Marks

**6.3 Quality Standards.** Partner's use of Marks and marketing must meet Company's quality standards.

**6.4 Ownership.** All intellectual property in Authorized Products remains Company's property. Partner acquires no ownership rights.

**6.5 Partner Materials.** Partner retains ownership of its independently developed materials, provided they do not infringe Company's IP.

**6.6 Open-Source Components.** If Authorized Products include open-source components (including AION-BRAIN):

(a) Such components remain under their open-source licenses

(b) Partner must comply with open-source license terms

(c) Partner must properly attribute open-source components
