# Reseller Agreement Template

Framework for organizations reselling AION-BRAIN services and solutions.

---

## Important Notice

**AION-BRAIN is open source under Apache License 2.0.**

This means:
- The core engines are **free** to use, modify, and distribute
- No license fee or reseller agreement is required for the open-source components
- This template is for organizations offering **value-added services** around AION-BRAIN

---

## What Requires Agreement

| Activity | Agreement Needed? |
|----------|-------------------|
| Using AION-BRAIN engines | No (Apache 2.0 license) |
| Modifying engines | No (Apache 2.0 license) |
| Including in your product | No (Apache 2.0 license) |
| Offering consulting services | No (but partnership encouraged) |
| Using "AION-Verified" badge | Yes (certification required) |
| Official partnership status | Yes (partner agreement) |
| Reselling premium services | Yes (when available) |

## Value-Added Services Framework

### What You Can Offer

| Service | Description | AION Agreement |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Implementation consulting** | Help clients deploy | Partner agreement recommended |
| **Training services** | Teach clients to use | Partner agreement recommended |
| **Custom development** | Build on AION-BRAIN | No agreement needed |
| **Managed services** | Operate for clients | No agreement needed |
| **Bundled solutions** | Include in your offering | Attribution required |

### Attribution Requirements

Per Apache 2.0 license, you must:
- Include the LICENSE file
- Include the NOTICE file
- Indicate any modifications made
- Not use AION trademarks to imply endorsement

## Partner Agreement Terms

### For Official Partners

If you want to be listed as an official AION-BRAIN partner:

**You Agree To:**

1. **Quality Standards**
   - Maintain high implementation quality
   - Follow implementation playbooks
   - Provide appropriate client support
   - Report issues to AION-BRAIN

2. **Ethical Standards**
   - Uphold AION principles
   - Not misrepresent capabilities
   - Include appropriate disclaimers
   - Protect client interests

3. **Training**
   - Complete partner training
   - Stay current on updates
   - Train your team appropriately

4. **Reporting**
   - Report implementation metrics
   - Share anonymized learnings
   - Provide client feedback

**AION-BRAIN Provides:**

1. **Partner Benefits**
   - Listing in partner directory
   - Partner training materials
   - Support access
   - Marketing materials

2. **Referrals**
   - Consideration for client referrals
   - Priority based on tier

3. **Support**
   - Partner support channel
   - Technical assistance
   - Escalation path

## Template Agreement Outline

*For organizations wanting formal partnership:*

### Article 1: Definitions

- "AION-BRAIN" refers to the open-source cognitive engine framework
- "Partner" refers to the organization entering this agreement
- "Services" refers to value-added services offered by Partner
- "End Clients" refers to Partner's customers

### Article 2: Grant of Rights

- Partner may offer Services using AION-BRAIN
- Partner may represent themselves as an official partner (if approved)
- Partner may use partner marketing materials (if provided)

### Article 3: Partner Obligations

- Maintain quality standards
- Complete required training
- Provide accurate reporting
- Follow ethical guidelines

### Article 4: AION-BRAIN Obligations

- Provide partner support
- Maintain partner directory listing
- Provide partner materials
- Consider for referrals

### Article 5: Term and Termination

- Initial term: 12 months
- Auto-renewal: Annual
- Termination: 30 days written notice
- Termination for cause: Immediate

### Article 6: Limitation of Liability

- AION-BRAIN open source provided "AS IS"
- Partner responsible for their Services
- No warranty beyond Apache 2.0 terms
- Mutual limitation of liability

### Article 7: General Provisions

- Governing law: [To be determined]
- Dispute resolution: [To be determined]
- Assignment: With consent
- Amendment: Written agreement
