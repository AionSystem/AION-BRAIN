## Template Usage Guide

### Step 1: Select Appropriate Template

Choose the template that most closely matches your intended use case:

| Use Case | Recommended Template |
|----------|---------------------|
| Confidential business discussions (both parties sharing) | Mutual NDA |
| Confidential discussions (you receiving only) | Unilateral NDA |
| Contributing code/documentation to AION-BRAIN | Contributor License Agreement |
| Academic research partnership | Research Collaboration Agreement |
| Handling personal data under GDPR | Data Processing Agreement |
| Healthcare data under HIPAA | Business Associate Agreement |
| Commercial enterprise deployment | Enterprise License Addendum |
| Professional consulting services | Professional Services Agreement |
| Co-developing new features/products | Joint Development Agreement |
| Reselling or integrating AION-BRAIN | Integration Partner Agreement |

### Step 2: Download and Customize

1. **Download** the template in your preferred format
2. **Replace** all bracketed placeholders `[PLACEHOLDER]` with actual information
3. **Review** all optional clauses and remove those not applicable
4. **Customize** jurisdiction-specific provisions for your location
5. **Add** any additional provisions required for your use case

### Step 3: Legal Review (MANDATORY)

Before using any template:

1. **Engage** a qualified attorney licensed in your jurisdiction
2. **Provide** complete context about intended use
3. **Request** review of all provisions for enforceability
4. **Document** any modifications recommended by counsel
5. **Obtain** written confirmation of legal adequacy

### Step 4: Execution

1. **Ensure** all parties have reviewed the final agreement
2. **Obtain** signatures from authorized representatives
3. **Maintain** executed copies for all parties
4. **Store** in secure, accessible location
