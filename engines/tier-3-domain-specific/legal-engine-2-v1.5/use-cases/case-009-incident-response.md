# Use Case 009: Compliance Incident Response Framework

**Engine:** Legal Engine 2 v1.5
**Domain:** Incident Management
**Complexity:** High
**Risk Level:** CRITICAL (regulatory reporting)

---

## Scenario Description

A compliance team needs to manage a compliance incident with potential regulatory reporting obligations. The engine provides framework guidance without specific legal advice.

---

## Input/Output Example

### Engine Response
```

======================================

[EDUCATIONAL FRAMEWORK - Legal Counsel Required]

⚠️ IF ACTIVE INCIDENT: Engage legal counsel immediately.
Time-sensitive regulatory reporting may apply.

────────────────────────

PHASE 1: DETECTION AND INITIAL RESPONSE
├─ Immediate Actions:
│   ├─ Document discovery circumstances
│   ├─ Preserve evidence
│   ├─ Notify designated incident lead
│   ├─ Engage legal counsel

├─ Initial Assessment:

│   ├─ Define scope
│   ├─ Identify investigation team
│   ├─ Establish privilege protocol
```
