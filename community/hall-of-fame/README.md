## Why We Recognize Contributors

AION-BRAIN is built by a community. Recognition serves multiple purposes:

1. **Gratitude** — Acknowledging the time and expertise people contribute
2. **Credibility** — Showing the expertise behind the project
3. **Motivation** — Encouraging continued contribution
4. **Transparency** — Being open about who builds what

## Nomination Process

### Self-Nomination
Made a contribution? Let us know:
1. Open a GitHub Discussion with "Recognition Request" tag
2. Describe your contribution
3. We'll review and add you to the appropriate category

### Nominate Others
Know someone who deserves recognition?
1. Open a GitHub Discussion with "Recognition Nomination" tag
2. Describe their contribution
3. We'll reach out to them for confirmation

---

## Recognition Criteria

### Minimum Requirements
- Meaningful contribution (not just typo fixes)
- Alignment with AION principles
- Positive community interaction

### Categories Explained

| Category | What Qualifies |
|----------|----------------|
| **Contributor** | Code, docs, translations, integrations |
| **Clinical Validator** | Healthcare professional who validates medical engine accuracy |
| **Legal Expert** | Legal professional who validates legal engine accuracy |
| **Scientific Contributor** | Researcher who contributes methodology or validation |
| **Security Researcher** | Security expert who identifies or fixes security issues |
| **Marketing Innovator** | Marketing professional advancing ethical AI marketing |
