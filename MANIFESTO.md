## Our Principles in Practice

### Contamination-Free Design
Every AION engine uses multi-pass independent analysis:
1. First pass: Generate hypothesis
2. Second pass: Challenge hypothesis (fresh context)
3. Third pass: Synthesize with full epistemic honesty

### Epistemic Humility Built-In
Every engine includes:
- Explicit uncertainty quantification
- Gödel-Turing constraint checking
- "What we don't know" sections
- Confidence decay over time

### Ethical Guardrails
Every engine includes:
- Misuse prevention protocols
- Stakeholder impact analysis
- Harm probability quantification
- "Will not recommend" boundaries

### Universal Accessibility
Every engine works for:
- PhD researchers AND high school students
- Silicon Valley founders AND rural teachers
- Western thinkers AND non-Western cultures
