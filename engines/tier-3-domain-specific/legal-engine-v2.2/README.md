# Legal Engine v2.2 — Hallucination-Hardened Legal Safeguards

**Classification:** TIER 3 — DOMAIN-SPECIFIC  
**Status:** Production Ready  
**Version:** 2.2

## Overview

Legal Engine v2.2 provides hallucination-hardened safeguards for attorneys using AI in legal practice. It substantially reduces malpractice risk through multi-layered prompt validation, post-generation verification, and comprehensive audit trails.

## Core Mission

Address the three critical risks every lawyer faces when using AI:

1. **Citation of non-existent authority** — AI frequently fabricates authentic-looking cases
2. **Confidentiality breaches** — Prompts may expose privileged information
3. **Improper delegation** — AI cannot replace attorney judgment
