# Crisis & Grief Engine - Training Materials

Professional development resources for crisis intervention and grief support.

---

## Training Overview

This engine is designed for use by trained professionals. These materials support proper implementation.

---

## Required Prerequisites

Before using this engine, professionals should have:

### Crisis Intervention
| Training | Provider Examples | Hours |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| Applied Suicide Intervention Skills Training (ASIST) | LivingWorks | 16 |
| Mental Health First Aid | MHFA | 8 |
| QPR (Question, Persuade, Refer) | QPR Institute | 2 |
| Crisis Text Line Training | Crisis Text Line | 30+ |

### Grief Counseling
| Training | Provider Examples | Hours |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| Grief Counseling Certification | ADEC, GCF | Varies |
| Complicated Grief Treatment | Columbia | 16+ |
| Thanatology Coursework | ADEC | Varies |

### Death Services
| Training | Provider Examples | Hours |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| Funeral Director Licensure | State Board | Varies |
| Death Doula Certification | INELDA, others | Varies |
