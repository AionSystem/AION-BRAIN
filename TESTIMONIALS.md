## Submit Your Testimonial

Used AION and it helped? Share your story:

### Option 1: GitHub Issue
1. Open issue with title: "Testimonial: [Brief description]"
2. Include:
   - Which engine(s) you used
   - What problem you solved
   - What insight you gained
   - (Optional) Your name/title for attribution

### Option 2: Email
Send to: AIONSYSTEM@outlook.com
Subject: "AION Testimonial"

### What We're Looking For

**Great testimonials include:**
- Specific engine used
- Concrete problem addressed
- Measurable or observable outcome
- How your thinking changed
- Would you recommend it?

**We especially want:**
- Diverse use cases (not just tech)
- Different industries
- Various expertise levels
- International perspectives
