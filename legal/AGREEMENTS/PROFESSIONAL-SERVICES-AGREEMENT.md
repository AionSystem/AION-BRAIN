**1.2 "Deliverables"** means all work product, materials, reports, and other items to be delivered to Client as specified in a Statement of Work.

**1.3 "Intellectual Property Rights"** means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and other intellectual property rights.

**1.4 "Pre-Existing Materials"** means any materials owned or licensed by a party prior to this Agreement or developed outside its scope.

**1.5 "Project"** means the specific engagement described in a Statement of Work.

**1.6 "Services"** means the professional services to be performed by Service Provider as described in a Statement of Work.

**1.7 "Statement of Work" or "SOW"** means a written document describing the Services, Deliverables, timeline, and fees for a specific Project.
