## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of training, attorneys will be able to:

1. **Understand AI Limitations**
   - Recognize hallucination risks
   - Identify citation fabrication patterns
   - Understand what AI cannot do

2. **Apply Verification Protocols**
   - Shepardize/KeyCite every citation
   - Verify holdings match summaries
   - Cross-check statutory currency

3. **Protect Confidentiality**
   - Identify PII before prompting
   - Apply redaction protocols
   - Understand disclosure risks

4. **Maintain Professional Responsibility**
   - Preserve attorney judgment
   - Document AI use appropriately
   - Supervise AI output

5. **Use Engine Features Effectively**
   - Select appropriate modes
   - Activate relevant modules
   - Generate proper audit trails
