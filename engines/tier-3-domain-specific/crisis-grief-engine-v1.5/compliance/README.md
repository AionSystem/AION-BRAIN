## Critical Compliance Reminders

### HIPAA Considerations
- Minimum necessary standard
- Authorization for disclosures
- Emergency exception clarity
- Documentation of disclosures
- Security of records

### Duty to Warn/Protect
- Jurisdiction-specific
- Identifiable victim vs. general threat
- Reasonable belief of danger
- Documentation of decision-making
- Consultation recommended

### Mandated Reporting
- Know your jurisdiction
- Suspect, not certainty required
- Timely reporting
- Documentation of report
- Immunity provisions (typically)

## Disclaimer

This compliance documentation provides general guidance only. It is not legal advice. Requirements vary by:
- State/jurisdiction
- Professional license type
- Setting (hospital, private practice, agency)
- Client population
- Specific circumstances

**Always consult:**
- Current regulations in your jurisdiction
- Your licensing board
- Legal counsel for specific situations
- Your employer's policies
- Your professional liability insurer
