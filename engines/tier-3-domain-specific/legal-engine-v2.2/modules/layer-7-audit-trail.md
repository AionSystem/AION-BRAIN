## Malpractice Defense Utility

This audit trail demonstrates:

### Reasonable Care Standard

- Attorney used validated prompt safety system
- Known risks identified and addressed before AI execution
- Industry best practices followed (ABA Opinion 512 compliance)
- Appropriate supervision of AI tool (Model Rule 5.3)

### Due Diligence

- Thorough pre-execution validation (8 layers applied)
- Post-generation verification required and documented
- Professional judgment preserved throughout process
- Client interests protected (confidentiality, competence)

### Professional Responsibility Compliance

- All applicable Model Rules reviewed and satisfied
- Ethical boundaries enforced automatically
- Jurisdiction-specific requirements considered
- Ongoing monitoring through verification protocol

### Good Faith Effort

- Proactive risk mitigation (not reactive after error)
- State-of-the-art safeguards employed
- Transparent limitations acknowledged
- Attorney maintains ultimate responsibility
