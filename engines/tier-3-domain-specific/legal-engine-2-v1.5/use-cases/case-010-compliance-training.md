# Use Case 010: Compliance Training Program Design

**Engine:** Legal Engine 2 v1.5
**Domain:** Compliance Training
**Complexity:** Medium
**Risk Level:** MEDIUM (regulatory expectation)

---

## Scenario Description

A compliance team needs to design or enhance their compliance training program. The engine provides framework guidance for training program development.

---
