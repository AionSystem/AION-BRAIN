## Why Legal Expertise Matters

Legal AI carries significant professional responsibility risks. Legal experts help ensure:

- **Accuracy** — Legal principles and precedents are correctly stated
- **Jurisdiction** — Multi-jurisdictional nuances are addressed
- **Ethics** — Professional responsibility rules are respected
- **Applicability** — Frameworks work in real legal practice

## How to Contribute

### Validation Process

1. **Express Interest**
   - Email: AIONSYSTEM@outlook.com
   - Subject: "Legal Expert Application"
   - Include: Bar admission(s), practice areas, experience

2. **Credential Verification**
   - We verify bar admission or equivalent
   - Confirm specialty expertise
   - Review conflict of interest

3. **Validation Assignment**
   - Receive specific engine components to review
   - Complete legal accuracy checklist
   - Provide written feedback with citations

4. **Recognition**
   - Listed on this page
   - Credited in engine documentation
   - CLE credit application (where applicable)
